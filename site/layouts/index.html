What is the Scrum Guide Expansion Pack?

Ken Schwaber and Jeff Sutherland created the Scrum framework, described in the 2020 Scrum Guide. To support widespread adoption, the guide was kept simple. This Scrum Guide Expansion Pack explains each element of Scrum; what it is, why it matters, and how it contributes to outcomes.
